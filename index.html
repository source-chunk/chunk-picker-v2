Patch Notes
11/17/2022 (5.1.0)

Major Changes & New Features

  • New Themes - Just in time for the end of Autumn, there is now an Autumn theme, alongside a Winter theme, that can enhance your chunk map! Try them out in the Setting window.
  • Notepad - There is now a notepad on the website, perfect for jotting down any notes you might have related to your map, both for yourself and for others to see! Access the notepad on the top bar, next to the Discord and Patreon icons.
  • New Tasks for This Chunk - Do you have a long list of tasks, and want to know what tasks are new every time you roll a chunk? Now you can know! Turn the option on in the Settings window and see any new tasks you have after rolling your next chunk.
  • Lock Mahogany Homes - When you unlock the Mahogany Homes minigame, and are assigned a task outside of your chunks, now you can lock those tasks from appearing until you gain access to that chunk. Try it out via the Activity Info window in the Mahogany Homes tab.

Other Changes & Bug Fixes

  • Changed the way the Shooting Star tab works to account for special requirements, like access to the Crafting Guild (thanks Frozen).
  • Added the ability to see what specific clue steps are doable in your chunk, via the Clues tab.
  • Added the ability to see what chunks are needed to complete all clues of a given tier, via the Clues tab (thanks Marstead).
  • Added customization for the Secondary Primary chunk rule, allowing you to specify how rare a drop can be and still count as primary for training purposes (thanks KingLagalot).
  • Added tasks for casting spells with blighted sacks (thanks Entrana Bro).
  • Added Thieving loot as part of the All Droptables and Every Drop chunk rules (thanks Chunky Beb)
  • Added a way to obtain POH stat-boosting teas.
  • Added a way to show multiple possible chunks for a quest in the quest panel (thanks Marstead).
  • Added collapsable headers for each monster for the Every Droptable rule to condense the amount of room it takes up on the task list (thanks KingLagalot).
  • Added a way to uncommonly get seeds from picking crops fields (thanks Chunk Nub).
  • Added a missing knife in the sack by the mage bank.
  • Added the mining glove variants and the fish sack barrel to the Fill Costume Room chunk rule.
  • Updated the droprate of the jar of darkness from the Poll changes.
  • Fixed the number of trees near the Fairy Ring south of Ardougne.
  • Fixed previously missing Woodcutting tasks in the Brimhaven Dungeon.
  • Fixed a bug where the area in Manual Areas weren't highlighted in green when unlocked.
  • Fixed the cattleprod being obtainable before starting Sheep Herder (thanks invalidCards).
  • Fixed a bug with noted drops and the All Droptables rule (thanks KingLagalot).
  • Fixed an issue with the number of guards in Falador (thanks Marstead).
  • Fixed the way that the Brimhaven Dungeon was being processed to now correctly account for your ability to access different sections of it.
  • Fixed a bug with ale yeast being obtainable without ecto-tokens (thanks ChunkiCinni).
  • Fixed the sorting with some Other Tasks in the task list.
  • Fixed an issue where skewering meat with an iron spit didn't require Firemaking (thanks Chunkifer).
  • Fixed a bug with a ghost variant near ZMI (thanks KingLagalot).
  • Fixed a bug with wiki links on quests with apostrophes.
  • Fixed a bug with processing a chocolate bar through Cooking before 40 Cooking (thanks Chunk Nub).
  • Fixed the task definition for making a chocolate cake to allow for chocolate dust too.
  • Fixed the list of items that can slash a web to include missing items.
  • Fixed Fishing Trawler giving incorrect requirements for players without primary Fishing training methods.

As always, if you experience any issues with your map or anything wrong related to this update, please contact me on Discord at whitecatblack#0890 or submit a bug report here.

Chunk Rolling Method
Roll 1 is the traditional method of rolling 1 random chunk as your next chunk. Roll 2 is an alternative way to roll, where 2 options are rolled randomly instead of 1, and then you pick which of those 2 to unlock! Roll 2 will let you influence your rolls slightly, but will still result in difficult rolls.
Chunk Rolling Style
Basic Rolling is the standard roll animation: no frills and quick. Fancy Rolling adds some flair to the roll, and builds suspense.
Color Scheme

Starting Chunk
Enter in the chunk ID of your starting chunk. This ID can be found in RuneLite, via the world map with the Region Locker plugin installed. (4 or 5 digits long)
If you haven't decided on your starting chunk yet, you can enter 0000 instead to skip this step; you'll be reminded next time you log in to set it again.
(The standard Lumbridge starting chunk is 12850)
If you ever need to change your starting chunk for whatever reason, you can do so via the settings menu in the top-right of the screen.
